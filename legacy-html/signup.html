Sign Up Instructions

Complete the USA Diving membership first, then choose the RecPlex option that matches how the athlete will practice with STL Diving. Monthly registration is $35/session. Drop-in practice is $40/session.

  • Monthly RecPlex registration: $35/session

Step 1: Complete USA Diving Membership

Start with the USA Diving athlete membership page. In the Athlete Memberships list, do not choose the $40 Athlete option or the $200 Competition Athlete option for normal STL Diving sign-up.

Scroll down to the two $22 introductory athlete choices and select the one that matches the diver's age:

17U Introductory Athlete$22 — for youth athletes
AQUA Age 18+ Introductory Athlete$22 — for adult athletes

Pick 17U for youth athletes. Pick AQUA Age 18+ for adult athletes. During checkout, USA Diving will ask if you want to affiliate the membership with a club — use the selections below.

USA Diving club affiliation selections

On the club affiliation screen, select these options:

AssociationMissouri Valley
ClubSTL Diving
Coach of RecordSamantha Mersnick or Peter Lucido

If the coach dropdown shows names individually, choose either Samantha Mersnick or Peter Lucido for STL Diving.

Step 3: Ask If You’re Unsure

If you are not sure whether monthly registration or drop-in practice is right for the athlete, ask before checking out. We can point you to the right RecPlex flow.
