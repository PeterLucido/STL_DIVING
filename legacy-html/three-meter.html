3-Meter Springboard

Coach-led 3-meter / board timing development.

3-meter gives divers more height, more spring, and a different timing rhythm. Coaches may introduce 3-meter drills and progressions when the athlete, drill, and practice setup make sense.

Why 3-meter matters

3-meter helps divers develop timing, confidence, air awareness, and stronger takeoffs. It is one part of the STL Diving team environment, introduced when the athlete, drill, and coach’s plan make sense.

STL Diving uses 3-meter to help athletes expand skill progressions, improve confidence with height, and prepare for competitive club opportunities where both 1-meter and 3-meter experience can matter.

Who this is for

Divers who are ready for coach-led 3-meter drills, timing work, stronger takeoffs, and board-specific progressions.

What divers work on

  • 3-meter approaches, hurdles, board control, and takeoff timing.
  • Front, back, inward, reverse, and twisting progressions.
  • Translating 1-meter fundamentals into higher-board skills.
  • Meet-list planning, consistency work, and pre-meet routines.
  • Dryland drills, flexibility, strength, and mental composure.

Progression goal

Divers work toward stronger air awareness, reliable competitive lists, and the confidence to train advanced springboard skills safely.

Training snapshot

Topic: 3-meter / board timing

How it works: Introduced through regular practice based on age, comfort, skill level, and safety readiness.
