Platform / Tower

Coach-led tower/platform training.

Tower/platform training is coach-led and board-specific. Athletes may be introduced through simple coach-led drills, jumps, entries, and progressions before higher-level platform skills are added.

Why platform matters

Coach-led platform work can help divers build confidence, body control, focus, and clean entries. It does not mean beginners are thrown onto high platforms; coaches choose appropriate heights, simple drills, and appropriate progressions.

STL Diving keeps platform development step-by-step. Divers may work through lower-height confidence, dryland preparation, entry control, and technical progressions before moving toward 5m, 7.5m, or 10m work.

Who this is for

Athletes who are ready for coach-led platform work at an appropriate height and difficulty level.

What divers work on

  • Platform safety rules, readiness checks, and confidence at height.
  • Step-by-step movement from lower platforms toward 5m, 7.5m, and 10m.
  • Takeoff positions, spotting, body line, and rip entries.
  • Dryland preparation, calm repetition, and patient skill building.
  • Mental focus, trust, and safe decision-making around higher boards.

Progression goal

The goal is not to rush height. Divers earn platform progress through consistent mechanics, confidence, and safe decision-making.
