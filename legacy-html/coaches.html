Coach / Mizzou Diver

Peter Lucido

Peter is a Francis Howell North standout, St. Louis Diving Club athlete, and University of Missouri diver with competitive experience across high school, USA Diving, age-group diving, NCAA Zone competition, and collegiate springboard/platform events.

  • Missouri state 1-meter champion senior year
  • University of Missouri swimming & diving athlete
  • NCAA Zone D competitor
  • Mizzou all-time top-ten performer on 1m and 3m
  • USA Diving Age Group Nationals finalist
  • Four-time GAC meet champion

Coach / South Dakota Diver

Samantha Mersnick

Samantha is a University of South Dakota diver from St. Charles, Missouri and Orchard Farm High School. She joined the USD swimming and diving staff as a graduate assistant after a four-year Coyote career.

  • Eight-time Summit League Championship finalist
  • Summit League 3-meter top-four finisher
  • Coyote Top Ten chart on both boards
  • Senior-season event winner
  • Summit League Peak Diver of the Week
  • USA Diving National Qualifier on 1m, 3m, and platform
